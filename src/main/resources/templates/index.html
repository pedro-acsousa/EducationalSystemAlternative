EPiC Learning Environment


Delivering Accessible Education To All

The Educational Practice in Computing are a group set on providing educational tools for everyone to access. We aim to give everyone the opportunity for proper education no matter their background, location or situation.

What our Platform Provides

The EPiC learning environment provides tools to:

  • - Enroll Students in Classes and Modules with others
  • - Set Students assignments, examinations and general work
  • - Allow students and teachers to check their progress
  • - Provide communication between Student and teacher
  • - Allow teachers to assess and give feedback to students work
  • - Easily create new student accounts and add them to the system

Download our Mobile App

Available on IOS and Android, the app provides:

  • Portable access to our system
  • Ability to check work and assignment on the go
  • Contact between students and teachers
